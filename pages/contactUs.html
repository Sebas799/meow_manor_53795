TERMS AND POLITICS

Welcome to Meow Manor, your premier destination for temporary cat lodging! We're delighted to offer a comfortable and safe environment for your beloved feline friends. Before booking your stay with us, please take a moment to review our policies and terms: Booking Confirmation: Your booking is confirmed once full payment is received. We accept payment via credit card or online transfer. Cancellation Policy: Cancellations made at least 48 hours before the scheduled check-in date will receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the check-in date will incur a 50% cancellation fee. Check-In and Check-Out: Check-in time is from 12:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Check-out time is by 12:00 PM on the departure date. Late check-outs may incur additional charges. Health and Vaccination Requirements: All cats must be up-to-date on vaccinations, including rabies and feline distemper. Proof of vaccination from a licensed veterinarian is required upon check-in. Behavior: We strive to provide a peaceful environment for all our guests. Aggressive or disruptive behavior from any cat may result in early termination of the stay without refund. Emergency Contact: Please provide an emergency contact number where you can be reached during your cat's stay with us. Liability: While every effort is made to ensure the safety and well-being of your cat, Meow Manor is not liable for any injuries, illnesses, or accidents that may occur during the stay. By booking a stay with us, you agree to abide by these policies and terms. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact us. We look forward to welcoming your furry friend to Meow Manor!
